* GALBA    D.II Records  and  papers concerning  England 
            and the Low  Countries, 1587. 
          Catalogues: 35213, fol. 40v (? this); 36789, fols 6, 155v, 
            163; 36682. 
          Fols 69v, 99v, 122v, 133y, 142v, 156v, ‘RC. 
          Previous ownership:  Fol. 308, art. 126, is noted (on fol. 
            308v) by Cotton: ‘Papers of my lord of Lecester I had of 
            Sir Arthur Aty and Sir John Dormer. Bundell 3’ Further 
            papers of Robert Dudley, earl of Leicester, and William 
            Herle in this volume  (and in others) may  be among 
            those that Cotton acquired from the same sources (see 
            also Galba C.VII, fol. 375v, following art. 141, and Galba 
            C.X, fol. 72v, art. 27). Fol. 207 in D.II may well be the 
            cover sheet to another of these papers (fols 208-10v, 
            art. 96). 
          Fols 263-66y, art. 111, are annotated by William Cecil. 
          Fols 179-80v, art. 82, are in the hand of a scribe who 
            worked  for Cotton. 
          Loan: Bulstrode Whitelocke, 1652 (231.23). 
          Organizational notes by Cotton. Annotations by Thomas 
            Cotton are on fol. 44, art. 19 (indicating that the arti- 
            cles concerned are misplaced) and fol. 356, art. 146. 
          Annexes  3d, 8e.
