VITELLIUS     C.XV Rochester Priory, Register of Acta. 
              Catalogues: 6018, no. 257 ; 36682 (entered as C.XV but 
                deleted). 
              This manuscript was repositioned as Faust. C.V, probably 
                at some  moment  in the seventeenth century. Smith, 
                Catalogue, notes that it had moved from the C.XV plac- 
                ing but does not state where to, although he has an 
                entry for it at Faust. C.V. There is no entry for Faust. C.V 
                in 36682.
