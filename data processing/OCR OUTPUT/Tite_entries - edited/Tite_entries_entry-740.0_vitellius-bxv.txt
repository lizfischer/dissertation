VITELLIUS     B.XV 
   Catalogue: a space at ‘B.15. is left in 36682 for an entry

and the 1656/7 checklist (fol. 1v) reads: ‘B.15.16.17. noe 
            Contents  in the Catalogue: nor overwritten. all wanting.' 
         There  is no entry for B.XV   in any other of the  early 
           catalogues and Smith, Catalogue, lists it as ‘Deest’. In a 
           letter of 1697 to Sir John Cotton, Smith again recorded 
           the absence of this manuscript and three.others: see the 
           entry for Vit. B.I.
