?* GALBA    A.III Sermons, part II; causa Regis Stephani 
       contra jus et titulum Matildis Imperatricis. 
     Catalogues: 6018, nos 109, 110, 388, 389 (see Galba A.II); 
       36789, fol. 10v; 36682. In each of these catalogues, the 
       ‘causa Regis Stephani’ is recorded as a part of Galba
