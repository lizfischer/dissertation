CLEOPATRA     A.XI Account of the expedition to Scotland, 
               1547. 
             Catalogues: 36789, fol. 33; 36682. 
             Previous ownership: George Ferrers, who was given the 
               manuscript by King Edward VI (fol. 3). The volume was 
               dedicated to the king. Ferrers had taken part in the
