   Westminster Abbey, pp. 27, 36-37, 51. Smith, Catalogue, 
   “To the Reader’, p. 24, notes the recovery of the manu- 
   scripts after 60 years’ absence. See also Teviotdale, 
   ‘Classified catalogues’, pp. 201, 205 n. 51.
