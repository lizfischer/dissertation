TIBERIUS     A.XI Register of St Mary's Abbey, Dublin. 
     Catalogue: 36682. 
     Previous ownership: James Ware, who  gave the manu- 
       script to Cotton in 1627 (Jul. C.III, fol. 386; Add. MS 
       4800, fol. 103v). 
     Richard James made   extracts from this manuscript in 
       Bodl., MS James  24 (SC 3861), pp. 25-27. A.XI (or 
       Bodl., MS Rawlinson B.495  (SC 11842)) may also be 
       the source of further extracts in MS James 17 (SC 3854), 
      p. 161.
