CLAUDIUS      A.XIII Cartulary of the Woodford family. 
              Catalogues: no entries. 
              Previous ownership:  ex dono Samuel  Roper of Heanor, 
                 1670 (fol. 1). 
              Listed by Thomas  Cotton, probably in the late 1630s, as 
                ‘wanting. 4°  (122.9), but A-XIII did not reach the 
                library until after his death in 1662. 
              Extracts, dated 1638, are in Bodl., MS Dodsworth 41 (SC 
                4183), fol. 105. 
              Annexes  3e, 5c.
