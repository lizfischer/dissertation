* GALBA    DVVIII Records and papers concerning England 
      and the Low  Countries, 1591. 
    Catalogues: 35213, fol. 40v (? this); 36789, fols 6, 155v, 
      163; 36682. 
    Previous ownership: The papers in this volume addressed 
      to Thomas   Bodley are frequently originals: this sug- 
      gests that Bodley may  himself have passed  these to 
      Cotton. 
    Papers in this volume may be among those sent to Cotton 
      by Ralph Starkey, possibly in 1625: Jul. C.II, fol. 362. 
    Fols 56v-58y, art. 19, fol. 138, art. 74, are in the hand of a 
      scribe who worked for Cotton. 
    Loan: Bulstrode Whitelocke, 1652 (231.28). 
    Organizational notes by Cotton. 
    Annex  8c.
