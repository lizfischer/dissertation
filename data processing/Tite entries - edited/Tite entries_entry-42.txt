JULIUS    D.X  Life of  Robert de  Bethune,  bishop of 
          Hereford; history of Lanthony Priory; etc. 
        Catalogues: 36789, fol. 156 (? this); 36682 (where entered 
          as D.XI). 
        Previous ownership: Thomas Talbot (fol. i); John Weever, 
          who  gave the manuscript to Cotton in 1628 (ex dono 
          inscription on fol. ii by Richard James). This ownership 
          is also recorded by Thomas Smith in his annotated copy 
          of his Catalogue: Bodl., MS Smith 140 (SC 15738).
