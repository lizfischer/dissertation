* GALBA   D.XII  Records and papers concerning England 
           and the Low Countries, 1596-1607. 
         Catalogues: 35213, fol. 40v (? this); 36789, fols 6, 155v, 
           163; 36682. 
         Previous ownership: The papers in this volume addressed 
           to Thomas Bodley  are originals: this suggests that 
           Bodley may himself have passed these to Cotton. 
         Papers in this volume may be among those sent to Cotton 
           by Ralph Starkey, possibly in 1625: Jul. C.III, fol. 362. 
         Fols 78-80, art. 34, fols 83v-85, art. 37, etc., are in the 
           hand of a scribe who worked for Cotton. 
         Loans: William Camden, 1612-16 (75.18: ? this); Bulstrode 
           Whitelocke, 1652 (231.32). 
         Organizational notes by Cotton. Fol. 87, art. 39, is anno- 
           tated by Thomas Cotton.
