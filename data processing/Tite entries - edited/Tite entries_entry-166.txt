?*  TIBERIUS     C.IX Life of  Richard IJ; cartulary of 
     Waltham  Abbey; etc. 
   Catalogue: 36682. 
   Previous ownership: ‘George Hilles Booke’, ‘Thomas Hill 
     his Booke / 1615’ (fol. 260). 
   Loan: John Selden, c.1638 (163.20). 
   There  are extracts ‘Ex Registro Monasterii de Waltham 
     capite 29. penes Rob. Cotton militem a° 1617’ in BL, 
     Stowe  MS  530, fol. 106. C.IX is the only Waltham 
     cartulary known to have been owned by Cotton but the 
     location in it of these extracts has yet to be established. 
   Extracts by Richard James  are in Bodl., MS James 24 
     (SC  3861), p. 58. Other extracts, probably made in 
     1639, are in Bodl., MS Dodsworth  78 (SC 5019), fol. 
     107v-15v. 
   Annex  7b.
