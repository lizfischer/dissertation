VITELLIUS E.IX Psalter. 
Catalogues: 36789, fol. 37; 36682. 
Listed in 6018, fol. 146v: ‘Vitellius E.9. 401. In 6018, no. 
401 is ‘Saxonice Evangeliven’ (sic), but this same 
description is repeated at no. 402, where it is correctly 
identified as Otho C.I. In all likelihood, therefore, E.TX 

was allotted the number 401 and should be regarded as 
listed in 6018.
